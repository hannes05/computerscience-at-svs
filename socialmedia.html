Social Media Safety

Quick tips on staying safe and respectful online — suitable for Year 7 students.

Explore content Take the assessment

What is Social Media?

Websites and apps that let people share, connect and talk — like Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube.

Common Risks

  • Unwanted contact
  • Sharing personal information
  • Cyberbullying
  • Inappropriate content

Quick Actions

Block, report, tell a trusted adult. Use strong privacy settings.

Get Help

If something worries you, tell a teacher or guardian, or visit Childline / CEOP.

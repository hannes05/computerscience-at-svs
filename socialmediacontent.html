What is Social Media?

Social media are apps and sites where people share posts, messages, photos and videos.

  • Examples: TikTok, Instagram, Snapchat, YouTube
  • People share content â€“ think before posting

Risks & How to Keep Safe

Cyberbullying

Don't reply to mean messages. Save evidence and tell a trusted adult.

Strangers & Messages

Never give out personal details or meet someone without an adult.

Inappropriate Content

If you see something upsetting, close the app and tell an adult.

Privacy Settings

Use private accounts and check who can see your posts.

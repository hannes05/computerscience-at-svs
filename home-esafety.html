Year 7 Computing – Digital Literacy & E-Safety

🧠 What You'll Learn

Welcome to the Digital Literacy & E-Safety unit! In this topic, you'll learn how to protect yourself and your data online, understand your digital rights, and become a responsible digital citizen.

  • Understand what personal data is and who holds it.
  • Learn about the Data Protection Act (2018) and GDPR.
  • Discover what identity theft is and how to prevent it.
  • Explore real-world data breaches and their consequences.
  • Learn how to stay safe on social media and protect your privacy.

🔒 Why It Matters

Every time you go online, you leave a digital footprint. Your personal information is valuable – to you and to criminals. Understanding how to protect your data and stay safe online is one of the most important skills you'll learn in computing.

Did you know? In 2020, British Airways was fined £20 million for a data breach that affected 500,000 customers!

Year 7 Computing – Operating Systems

🧠 What You’ll Learn

Welcome to the Operating Systems unit! In this topic, you’ll learn what an operating system is, why it’s essential, and how it allows you to interact with your computer, phone, or tablet.

  • Understand the difference between hardware and software.
  • Explore how operating systems manage devices, files, and memory.
  • Discover different types of operating systems and interfaces (GUI vs CLI).
  • Learn what happens when your computer boots up.

💻 Why It Matters

Every digital device you use — from your laptop to your smartwatch — relies on an operating system. Understanding how it works helps you troubleshoot problems, stay safe online, and think like a computer scientist.
